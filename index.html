Why PeerDrop

Built for people who care about privacy and speed.

🔒

End-to-end encrypted

Every transfer uses the Noise protocol with Curve25519 and ChaCha20-Poly1305. Unreadable to anyone except the recipient.

⚡

No cloud, no waiting

Files go directly device to device over Hyperswarm. No upload then download — pure peer-to-peer at full network speed.

🖥

Lives in your menu bar

One click, see who's online, drop the file. PeerDrop stays out of your way and appears exactly when you need it.

🔑

No accounts ever

Your identity is a cryptographic key stored only on your device. Nothing to sign up for, nothing to log into.

📱

Cross-platform

Send from your MacBook to your iPhone, Android, or Linux machine. One identity works across all your devices automatically.

📂

Any file, any size

Drag a single photo or an entire project folder. PeerDrop handles files and directories of any size without limits.

How it works

Three steps. First time only.

1

Share your Peer ID

Open Settings, copy your Peer ID and send it to whoever you want to connect with. Just once, forever.

2

Connect automatically

PeerDrop uses Hyperswarm to find your contacts across any network — home, office, cellular. No configuration.

3

Drop to send

Drop any file onto their panel in your menu bar. It arrives on their machine in seconds, encrypted all the way.

Transfers

Watch it fly.

Real-time progress, right inside the send panel. Queue more files while the current one is in flight.

  • Progress bar per file
  • Queue multiple files at once
  • Native macOS notifications on completion
  • Files land in your Downloads/PeerDrop folder
Identity

One key.
Every device.

PeerDrop derives your identity from a mnemonic seed. The same seed on multiple devices means they all share one Peer ID — your own devices appear in "My Devices" automatically.

  • Cryptographic identity, no account needed
  • Same ID across all your Macs, iPhones and Android devices
  • Copy or QR-share your Peer ID from Settings
  • Seed stored locally, never leaves your device
